Hello and welcome. It’s an honor to share my love of yoga with you. I’m Kimberly, a Certified Iyengar Yoga Teacher, and “KZaM” is a whimsical nickname that’s short for my real name: Kimberly Zanger Mackesy.

I go by “KZaM” because I believe in magic. Not magic where you wave a wand and boom! your wish is granted, but the real kind of everyday magic that involves effort, letting go, and a hearty dash of mystery. A child’s first steps. An ace performance, delivered. Balancing in a headstand for the first time. Life’s magical moments are created over time and realized in an instant; to me, creating these moments is what Yoga is all about.

I invite you to explore this site, get in touch, and find out more about how you can bring real yoga magic into your life.

With love, Kimberly Zanger Mackesy, CIYT
